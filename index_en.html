About Me

As a Backend & Machine Vision Engineer, I have 3 years of practical experience in backend development, vision software, and hardware integration systems. Driven by a passion for computer science since childhood, I entered university as a software talent scholar and graduated early as a National Science & Engineering Scholar.

During my undergraduate studies, I gained practical experience by working in research labs and companies, leading various projects from start to finish in diverse fields such as semiconductor inspection and e-commerce.

Notably, the vision inspection project I led at Deepseers was selected for the Ministry of SMEs and Startups' TIPS and DIPS R&D programs, recognized for both its technical excellence and market potential. I have also explored the AI field by researching memory-efficient GAN frameworks and filing a patent for deep learning-based ROI recommendation technology.

Additionally, through my service as a KATUSA at the USFK Headquarters, I gained experience in multinational personnel data management and collaboration, equipping me with the ability to work proactively in global environments.
